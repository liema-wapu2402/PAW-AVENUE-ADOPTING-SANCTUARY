Meet Our Veterinary Doctor

Doc Nielsen Donato

Lincensed Veterinarian

Doc Nielsen has genuine love and compassion for animals. As the managing partner and chief surgeon of Vets in Practice (VIP), he tends to different kinds of pets every day. He also runs the Laguna Wildlife Park & Rescue Center in Pansol, where various animals are sheltered, including stray dogs.

Doc Ferds Recio

Lincensed Veterinarian

Doc Ferdinand “Ferds” Recio is a skilled in Veterinary Medicine, Human and animal health, TV hosting, Conducting seminars, Webinars. Graduated from the University of the Philippines worked in Dubai, US and Canada and travelled the world for work and experience.

Doc. Joy Vinluan

Lincensed Veterinarian

Dr. Joy Vinluan, a seasoned veterinarian, specializes in general practice, focusing on the well-being of dogs and cats. She brings her expertise and passion for animal care to Vetlife Animal Clinic in Pasay City, where she diligently attends to her patients' needs with compassion and dedication.

Doc. Racky Velasquez.

Lincensed Veterinarian

Dr. Racky is renowned in the animal world, celebrated for his expertise and preparedness. With exceptional skills and a warm bedside manner, he tends to his patients at Mount Sinai Veterinary Practice, earning admiration throughout the community for his dedication to animal care.
